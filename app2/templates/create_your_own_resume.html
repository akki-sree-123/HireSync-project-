What is LaTeX?

LaTeX is a high-quality typesetting system designed for the production of technical and scientific documentation. Unlike conventional word processors, LaTeX uses markup tags to define the structure and styling of your document, allowing you to focus on content rather than formatting.

LaTeX excels at:

  • Mathematical equations and scientific notation
  • Precise control over document layout and spacing
  • Automated cross-references, citations, and bibliographies
  • Consistent styling across large documents
  • Creating publication-ready professional documents

Overleaf provides a user-friendly web interface to LaTeX, making it accessible even to those with no prior LaTeX experience.
